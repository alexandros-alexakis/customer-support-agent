# Sample Conversation - Immediate Escalation

## Scenario

A player contacts support claiming their account was accessed by 
someone else and in-game items are missing.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
Someone hacked into my account. I just logged in and all my resources 
are gone. I did not give anyone my password. Please help.

**Agent:**
Hi, I am sorry to hear this happened. I understand this is very 
upsetting and I want to assure you this is being taken seriously.

To protect your account right now, please do the following immediately:
1. Change your password from a secure device.
2. If your account is linked to a Google or Facebook account, 
   change those passwords as well.
3. Check for any apps or services connected to your account and 
   revoke access to any you do not recognize.

Have you been able to change your password already?

**Player:**
Yes I just changed it. But my resources are still gone.

**Agent:**
Thank you for acting quickly. I am escalating this to our security 
team immediately as this requires specialist investigation. They will 
review your account activity and look into the missing resources.

To help them investigate, could you confirm:
1. Your Player ID or in-game username
2. Approximately when you last had normal access to your account
3. What device you normally play on

**Player:**
My username is StarCommander7. I last played yesterday evening, 
everything was fine. I play on iPhone 13.

**Agent:**
Thank you. I have logged all of this and your case is being escalated 
as high priority to our security team. You can expect a follow-up 
within 4 hours.

Please do not share your new password with anyone, including support 
staff. We will never ask for it.

I am sorry this happened and we will do everything we can to 
investigate. Is there anything else you need right now?

**Player:**
No, just please fix this as soon as possible.

**Agent:**
Understood. Our team will be in touch shortly. Thank you for your 
patience.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| Tone | Urgent acknowledgment, calm and reassuring |
| Tier 1 steps followed | Immediate security advice given first |
| Information collected | Username, timeline, device |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - immediately, high priority |
| Promises made | None beyond timeframe and investigation |
| Outcome | Correctly routed to security team |
