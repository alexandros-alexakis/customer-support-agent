# Sample Conversation - Angry Player / De-escalation

## Scenario

A player contacts support for the third time about an unresolved payment issue. They are frustrated and hostile.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
This is absolutely unacceptable. I have contacted you THREE times about my missing purchase and nobody has done anything. I want my money back NOW. This is theft.

**Agent:**
I completely understand your frustration, and I sincerely apologize that this has taken so long to resolve. Being contacted three times without a resolution is not the experience we want for you, and I take full responsibility for making sure this gets addressed properly today.

Could you please share your Player ID and the ticket numbers from your previous contacts so I can review the full history of your case right now?

**Player:**
My ID is WarriorKing55. I don't have the ticket numbers, nobody gave them to me.

**Agent:**
Thank you. I can look up your case history using your Player ID. Please give me a moment to review everything that has happened so far.

[Reviews case history]

I can see your previous contacts and I can see this has not been resolved. I am escalating this now as an urgent case directly to our billing specialist team with a note flagging the repeated contacts and the urgency. I will not let this go into a general queue.

You will receive a direct response within 4 hours. I am also adding a priority flag to ensure this is not delayed further.

**Player:**
You people always say that and nothing happens.

**Agent:**
I understand why you don't trust that right now - you have been told things before and they didn't happen. I can't undo that, but I can tell you exactly what I have done: your case is now flagged as urgent with your full contact history attached. The billing team will see all three contacts when they review it.

If you do not hear back within 4 hours, please reply to this ticket and I will personally escalate further.

**Player:**
Fine. 4 hours.

**Agent:**
Thank you for your patience. I will make sure this is resolved for you.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| Tone | Absorbed hostility without matching it, stayed calm and took ownership |
| Empathy | Acknowledged the repeated contact failure directly, did not deflect |
| Information collected | Player ID, case history retrieved internally |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - urgent priority, flagged as repeat contact |
| Promises made | Only timeframe and follow-up commitment, no outcome promise |
| Outcome | Player de-escalated, case prioritized correctly |
