# Sample Conversation - Technical Issue

## Scenario

A player contacts support because the game crashes every time they try to load into a specific in-game battle.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
Every time I try to start a battle the game crashes. It has been happening for 2 days. I've lost resources because of this.

**Agent:**
I'm sorry to hear this is happening - losing resources due to a crash is genuinely frustrating and I want to help get this resolved.

To help me investigate, could you please tell me:
1. What device and operating system are you using?
2. Is the game updated to the latest version?
3. Does the crash happen in all battles or only specific ones?

**Player:**
I'm on Samsung Galaxy S21, Android 13. Yes it's updated. It only happens in Alliance Wars battles.

**Agent:**
Thank you, that is very helpful. The fact that it only happens in Alliance Wars battles suggests this may be a specific issue with that feature rather than your device.

Could you please try the following steps:
1. Close the game fully and clear the app cache.
2. Restart your device.
3. Try to start an Alliance Wars battle again and let me know if the crash still occurs.

**Player:**
I tried that, still crashing.

**Agent:**
Thank you for trying. Since the issue persists after a cache clear and restart, and is specific to Alliance Wars, I am escalating this to our technical team for investigation.

Your details:
- Device: Samsung Galaxy S21, Android 13
- Issue: Crash on Alliance Wars battle load
- Steps already attempted: Cache clear, restart, app is up to date

Regarding the resources lost, I have noted this in your ticket. Resource restoration for technical issues is reviewed alongside the bug investigation.

You can expect a follow-up within 24-48 hours. Is there anything else I can help with?

**Player:**
No thanks, hope it gets fixed.

**Agent:**
Our team will look into it. Thank you for the detailed information - it helps us investigate faster.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| Tone | Empathetic, practical |
| Tier 1 steps followed | Yes - cache clear and restart attempted before escalating |
| Information collected | Device, OS, app version, specific scenario, steps attempted |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - bug specific to a game feature after standard steps failed |
| Resource loss noted | Yes - flagged without making restoration promise |
| Outcome | Correctly escalated with full technical details |
