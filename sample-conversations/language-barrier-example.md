# Sample Conversation - Language Barrier

## Scenario

A player contacts support in broken English. They are struggling to explain their issue clearly but appear to have a payment problem.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
Hello i buy coins yesterday but no coins in game. I pay money. Please help me i dont understand why.

**Agent:**
Hello, thank you for contacting us. I understand - you paid for coins but they did not appear in your game. I will help you.

Please answer these questions:
1. What is your Player ID or username in the game?
2. How much did you pay?
3. Do you have a receipt or transaction number from your payment?

Take your time - short answers are fine.

**Player:**
My name in game is RedStar88. I pay 9.99. I have number from email: TXN-554821

**Agent:**
Thank you, RedStar88. I have your information:

- Player: RedStar88
- Amount: 9.99
- Transaction: TXN-554821

Please try this first:
1. Close the game completely
2. Wait 1 minute
3. Open the game again
4. Check your coins

Did the coins appear?

**Player:**
I try. No coins still. I am angry because I pay real money.

**Agent:**
I understand. You paid real money and received nothing - that is not acceptable and I am sorry this happened.

I am now sending your case to our payment team. They will check your transaction and fix this.

Your reference number is TXN-554821. They will contact you within 24 hours.

Is there anything else you need?

**Player:**
No thank you. I hope fix fast.

**Agent:**
We will fix it. Thank you for your patience.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| Language adaptation | Short sentences, simple vocabulary, no idioms or jargon |
| Tone | Patient, warm, no condescension |
| Information collection | Simplified to essentials, explicitly said short answers are fine |
| Confirmation technique | Repeated back collected info in a simple list for clarity |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - payment not received after restart |
| Outcome | Player understood the process, felt heard despite language barrier |

## Key Principles for Language Barrier Interactions

- Use short sentences
- One instruction at a time
- Avoid idioms, slang, and complex vocabulary
- Repeat back what you understood to confirm accuracy
- Never correct the player's grammar or comment on their English
- Show extra patience - frustration is amplified when you cannot express yourself clearly
