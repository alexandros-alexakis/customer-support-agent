# Sample Conversation - Payment Issue

## Scenario

A player contacts support claiming they were charged for a purchase 
but did not receive the in-game currency they bought.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
Hi, I just bought 5000 gold coins but they never appeared in my account. 
I was charged on my card. This is really frustrating.

**Agent:**
Hi, thank you for reaching out. I completely understand how frustrating 
that is, especially when a payment has already gone through. Let me help 
you get this sorted.

Could you please provide me with the following:
1. Your Player ID or in-game username
2. The transaction ID or receipt from your payment provider
3. The date and approximate time of the purchase

**Player:**
My username is DragonKing99. I bought it about an hour ago. 
Here is my transaction ID: TXN-884521.

**Agent:**
Thank you for that information. While I look into this, could you also 
try the following steps in case it is a delivery delay:

1. Close the app completely.
2. Wait 60 seconds and reopen it.
3. Check your gold coin balance again.

Please let me know if the coins appear after doing this.

**Player:**
I tried that, still nothing.

**Agent:**
Thank you for checking. Since the purchase is confirmed but the items 
have not been delivered after a restart, I am escalating this to our 
billing specialist team for manual verification and resolution.

Your case has been logged with transaction ID TXN-884521 and will be 
reviewed within 24 hours. You will receive a follow-up directly.

I apologize for the inconvenience and appreciate your patience. Is 
there anything else I can help you with in the meantime?

**Player:**
No that's fine, thank you.

**Agent:**
You're welcome. We will be in touch shortly. Have a great day.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| Tone | Empathetic opening, professional throughout |
| Tier 1 steps followed | Yes - restart attempted before escalating |
| Information collected | Username, transaction ID, timing |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - items missing after troubleshooting |
| Promises made | None - only timeframe provided |
| Outcome | Correctly escalated to billing specialist |
