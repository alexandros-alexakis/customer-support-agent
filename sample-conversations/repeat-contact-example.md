# Sample Conversation - Repeat Contact

## Scenario

A player contacts support for the second time about a login issue that was supposedly resolved in a previous ticket.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
I contacted you last week about not being able to log in. The agent told me it was fixed but I still can't log in on my tablet. It works on my phone but not my tablet.

**Agent:**
I'm sorry to hear the issue wasn't fully resolved after your first contact. I can see your previous ticket and I understand this must be frustrating.

Let me look into the tablet-specific issue. A few quick questions:
1. What type of tablet are you using and what OS version?
2. Are you using the same login method on the tablet as on your phone (Google, Facebook, Apple ID, email)?
3. What error message, if any, appears when you try to log in on the tablet?

**Player:**
It's an iPad, iOS 16. Same Google account. It just says 'authentication failed'.

**Agent:**
Thank you. An 'authentication failed' error on a specific device while another device works suggests this may be a session or token issue on the iPad.

Please try the following:
1. On the iPad, go to Settings > Privacy > tap on the game > check that all permissions are enabled.
2. Open the game, log out completely, and then log back in fresh.
3. If that fails, delete the app, reinstall it, and try logging in again.

**Player:**
I deleted and reinstalled, still authentication failed.

**Agent:**
Thank you for trying. Since this is a second contact on the same account and the reinstall hasn't resolved it, I am escalating this to our account specialist team as a priority case.

I have attached your previous ticket history so the specialist team has full context and you won't need to explain this again. You can expect a response within 24 hours.

I apologize that this wasn't resolved on your first contact.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| Repeat contact handling | Acknowledged immediately, reviewed previous ticket |
| Tier 1 steps followed | Yes - device-specific troubleshooting before escalating |
| Information collected | Device, OS, login method, error message |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - second contact + reinstall failed |
| Previous ticket attached | Yes - avoided making player repeat themselves |
| Outcome | Correctly escalated with full history |
