# Sample Conversation - Fraud Suspicion

## Scenario

A player contacts support claiming another player is using cheats or exploiting the game to gain an unfair advantage.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
There is a player in my server called DragonLord999 who is clearly cheating. Their resource levels are impossible - they went from rank 200 to rank 1 overnight. This is ruining the game for everyone.

**Agent:**
Thank you for reporting this. We take fair play seriously and all reports are investigated thoroughly.

To help our team review this properly, could you please provide:
1. Your Player ID
2. The reported player's exact in-game name or Player ID if you have it
3. A description of what you observed and when (approximate date and time)
4. Any screenshots if you were able to capture them

**Player:**
My ID is SilverKnight42. Their name is DragonLord999. It happened on the night of April 12th. I have some screenshots.

**Agent:**
Thank you. I have logged your report with the following details:

- Reporter: SilverKnight42
- Reported player: DragonLord999
- Incident: Suspected exploit or unauthorized advancement
- Date: April 12th

Please attach your screenshots to this ticket by replying with them attached - they will significantly support the investigation.

I am escalating this to our Trust and Safety team who handle all fair play investigations. Please note that for privacy reasons we cannot share the outcome of any action taken against another player's account, but all reports are reviewed.

Thank you for helping keep the game fair for everyone.

**Player:**
Ok thanks. Will anything actually happen?

**Agent:**
All reports are reviewed by our Trust and Safety team. I can't share details of any action taken on another player's account, but your report will be investigated. The screenshots you provide will be helpful.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| Tone | Took the report seriously without over-promising action |
| Information collected | Reporter ID, reported player name, date, screenshots requested |
| Privacy maintained | Did not promise to share outcome of investigation |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - Trust and Safety team |
| Expectation management | Clear about what can and cannot be shared |
| Outcome | Report correctly logged and escalated |
