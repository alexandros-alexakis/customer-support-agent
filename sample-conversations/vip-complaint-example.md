# Sample Conversation - VIP Player Complaint

## Scenario

A high-spend VIP player contacts support claiming they did not receive a limited-time exclusive item they purchased as part of a special bundle.

---

## Conversation

**Player:**
I purchased the Warlord's Bundle yesterday for $99.99 and the exclusive skin was not in my inventory. I spend a lot on this game and I expect this to be fixed immediately.

**Agent:**
Thank you for reaching out. I can see this is an important issue and I want to make sure it is handled correctly for you. I apologize that the item was not delivered as expected.

Could you please confirm your Player ID and share your transaction receipt or ID so I can review your purchase?

**Player:**
My ID is CommanderElite. Transaction ID is TXN-229847.

**Agent:**
Thank you. I have located your account and I can see the purchase was processed successfully. I am escalating this immediately to our billing specialist team as a priority case to ensure the item is reviewed and delivered without further delay.

Given the nature of this purchase, I want to make sure you receive a direct follow-up within 2 hours rather than the standard timeframe. I have flagged your case accordingly.

Is there anything else I can assist you with while this is being reviewed?

**Player:**
No, just get it sorted. I've been playing for 3 years and spending consistently - I don't expect to chase up purchases.

**Agent:**
That is completely fair, and your loyalty to the game is appreciated. This should not have required you to follow up. Our specialist team will be in touch within 2 hours.

---

## Analysis

| Element | Notes |
|---|---|
| VIP identification | Identified through high-value purchase, treated accordingly |
| Tone | Professional, elevated warmth without being sycophantic |
| Response speed | Immediate escalation, reduced timeframe communicated |
| Information collected | Player ID, transaction ID |
| Escalation triggered | Yes - priority, flagged as VIP |
| Promises made | Timeframe only, no restoration promise |
| Outcome | Player acknowledged, case escalated correctly |
