# FAQ - Account Access and Login

## Overview

This document covers the most common account access and login issues 
reported by players at Tier 1 support level.

---

## Q1: A player cannot log into their account.

**Likely causes:**
- Incorrect credentials
- Session timeout or token issue
- Server-side authentication issue

**Resolution steps:**
1. Ask the player which login method they use (email, Facebook, 
   Google, Apple ID, guest).
2. Ask them to confirm they are using the correct credentials.
3. Ask them to try logging out fully and logging back in.
4. Ask them to clear app cache and retry.
5. If the issue persists, collect account details and escalate.

**Do not** ask for passwords. Ever.

---

## Q2: A player forgot their password.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Direct them to the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen.
2. Confirm which email address is associated with the account.
3. Ask them to check spam/junk folders for the reset email.
4. If the reset email is not arriving, collect their email address 
   and escalate to account team.

---

## Q3: A player lost access to their guest account.

**This is a high-sensitivity issue.** Guest accounts are not tied 
to an email or social login, making recovery difficult.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Ask if they have a Player ID or any in-game screenshots showing 
   their account.
2. Ask what device they were playing on and whether they still have 
   access to it.
3. Escalate immediately with all collected details.
4. Set clear expectations: recovery is not guaranteed without 
   sufficient identifying information.

---

## Q4: A player's account was logged into by someone else / they 
suspect unauthorized access.

**Treat as high priority.**

**Resolution steps:**
1. Advise the player to change their password immediately if they 
   still have access.
2. Advise them to revoke any connected third-party app permissions.
3. Escalate to the security team immediately with account details 
   and a description of the suspected breach.
4. Do not investigate account access logs at Tier 1 level.

---

## Q5: A player wants to merge two accounts or transfer progress.

**Policy:**
Account merges and progress transfers are not handled at Tier 1.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Acknowledge the request.
2. Escalate to account specialist team with details of both accounts.
3. Inform the player that this process is reviewed case by case and 
   set realistic timeframe expectations.

---

## Q6: A player is being logged out repeatedly mid-session.

**Likely causes:**
- Unstable internet connection
- Session token expiry bug
- Device-specific issue

**Resolution steps:**
1. Ask the player to check their internet connection stability.
2. Ask them to clear cache and reinstall if the issue persists.
3. Collect device model, OS version, and frequency of the issue.
4. If multiple players report the same issue simultaneously, 
   escalate as a potential server-side bug.
