# Seasonal Events Guide

## Overview

In-game seasonal events (holiday events, anniversaries, limited-time content) generate significant ticket volume spikes. This guide prepares agents to handle event-related issues effectively.

---

## Common Event-Related Issues

### Issue 1: Event rewards not received

**Resolution steps:**
1. Confirm the player meets the eligibility criteria for the reward.
2. Ask them to check all inventory locations - rewards sometimes appear in secondary tabs.
3. Ask them to restart the app.
4. If rewards are still missing after restart and eligibility is confirmed, escalate with Player ID and event name.

**Do not** restore rewards at Tier 1 level.

---

### Issue 2: Event content not appearing

**Resolution steps:**
1. Confirm the event is currently active (check internal event calendar).
2. Ask the player to update the app to the latest version.
3. Ask them to clear cache and restart.
4. If the event is confirmed active and the player meets requirements but still cannot access it, escalate to technical team.

---

### Issue 3: Player missed event rewards due to a bug

**Resolution steps:**
1. Collect Player ID, description of the bug, and approximate time it occurred.
2. Escalate to the technical team with high priority.
3. Inform the player that missed rewards due to confirmed bugs are reviewed for restoration.
4. Do not confirm restoration until specialist team confirms eligibility.

---

### Issue 4: Player confused about event mechanics

**Resolution steps:**
1. Explain the event mechanics clearly based on available documentation.
2. Direct the player to in-game event guides or official social channels for detailed information.
3. If the mechanics are genuinely unclear or appear to be working incorrectly, escalate.

---

## Volume Management During Events

- Notify the team lead immediately if ticket volume spikes significantly during an event.
- Use the incident response playbook if the volume indicates a widespread issue.
- Prioritize tickets from players reporting bugs over general mechanics questions during high-volume periods.
