# FAQ - Refund Policy

## Overview

This document provides a detailed decision tree for handling refund requests at Tier 1 level. Refund decisions are never made at Tier 1 - this document exists to help agents collect the right information and route correctly.

---

## Refund Request Decision Tree

### Step 1: Identify the refund type

| Type | Description | Route |
|---|---|---|
| Failed purchase - charged but no item | Payment processed, item not delivered | Billing specialist |
| Duplicate charge | Player charged twice for same item | Billing specialist - urgent |
| Unauthorised charge | Player did not make the purchase | Security team + Billing specialist |
| Change of mind | Player no longer wants the item | Billing specialist - low priority |
| Technical issue during purchase | App crashed mid-purchase | Billing specialist |

---

### Step 2: Collect required information

Regardless of refund type, always collect:

- Player ID
- Transaction ID or receipt
- Date and approximate time of purchase
- Payment method (card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, etc.)
- Amount charged
- What was purchased
- What was or was not received

---

### Step 3: Set correct expectations

**Always say:**
"Refund eligibility is reviewed case by case by our billing team. I cannot confirm whether a refund will be approved, but your case will be reviewed."

**Never say:**
- "You will get a refund"
- "We don't do refunds"
- "I'm sure they'll sort it out"
- Any specific timeline beyond the standard review window

---

## Refund Eligibility Factors

These are reviewed by the billing specialist team, not Tier 1. Provided here for agent awareness only:

| Factor | Notes |
|---|---|
| Time since purchase | Most platforms have a refund window (e.g. 48 hours) |
| Whether item was used | Consumed items are generally non-refundable |
| Purchase history | First-time refund requests treated differently to repeat requests |
| Nature of issue | Technical failures weighted more favourably than change of mind |
| Platform policy | Apple, Google, and direct purchases have different rules |

---

## Platform-Specific Notes

| Platform | Refund Process |
|---|---|
| Apple App Store | Refunds processed by Apple, not the game. Direct player to reportaproblem.apple.com |
| Google Play | Refunds within 48 hours can be self-served via Google Play. After 48 hours, escalate to billing |
| Direct purchase | Handled entirely by the billing specialist team |

---

## Escalation Checklist for Refund Requests

Before escalating, confirm you have collected:

- [ ] Player ID
- [ ] Transaction ID
- [ ] Purchase date and amount
- [ ] Platform (Apple, Google, direct)
- [ ] Description of issue
- [ ] Whether item was received or used
- [ ] Player's preferred resolution (refund vs item delivery)
