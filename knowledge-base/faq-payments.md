# FAQ - Payments and Purchases

## Overview

This document covers the most common payment and purchase issues 
reported by players at Tier 1 support level.

---

## Q1: A player was charged but did not receive their purchased items.

**Likely causes:**
- Transaction processed but delivery delayed
- App was closed before delivery completed
- Temporary server-side issue

**Resolution steps:**
1. Ask the player to restart the app and check again.
2. Ask for the transaction ID or receipt from their payment provider.
3. Verify the purchase against available order records.
4. If items are still missing after restart, escalate with transaction ID attached.

**Do not** promise manual delivery without verification.

---

## Q2: A player's payment failed but they were charged.

**Likely causes:**
- Bank pre-authorization that was not released
- Double charge due to a retry attempt

**Resolution steps:**
1. Advise the player to check with their bank - pre-authorizations 
   typically release within 3-5 business days.
2. If the charge is confirmed and no purchase exists on the account, 
   escalate to billing specialist immediately.
3. Collect: transaction ID, date, amount, payment method type.

**Do not** issue refund commitments at Tier 1.

---

## Q3: A player wants a refund.

**Policy:**
Refunds are not handled at Tier 1 level.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Acknowledge the request professionally.
2. Escalate to billing team with full transaction details.
3. Inform the player that refund eligibility is reviewed case by case 
   and provide expected response timeframe.

**Do not** indicate whether a refund will or will not be approved.

---

## Q4: A player's purchase option is not showing or the store is not loading.

**Likely causes:**
- App cache issue
- Regional store availability
- Temporary backend issue

**Resolution steps:**
1. Ask the player to clear app cache and restart.
2. Ask what device and OS version they are using.
3. If the issue persists across multiple restarts, collect device info 
   and escalate to technical team.

---

## Q5: A player was charged multiple times for the same item.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Treat as high priority.
2. Collect all transaction IDs and dates.
3. Escalate immediately to billing specialist.
4. Inform the player their case is being reviewed urgently.

**Do not** attempt to resolve duplicate charges at Tier 1.
