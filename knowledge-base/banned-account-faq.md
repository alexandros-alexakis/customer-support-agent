# FAQ - Banned and Suspended Accounts

## Overview

Account bans and suspensions are among the most emotionally charged tickets in gaming support. This document defines how to handle them at Tier 1 level.

**Important:** Tier 1 agents do not review, lift, or modify bans. All ban-related cases are escalated to the Trust and Safety team.

---

## Types of Account Restrictions

| Type | Description | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Temporary suspension | Account access restricted for a defined period | Hours to days |
| Permanent ban | Account permanently disabled | Permanent |
| Chat ban | Player can play but cannot use in-game chat | Varies |
| Feature restriction | Specific feature disabled (e.g. trading, alliance) | Varies |

---

## Q1: A player says their account has been banned and they don't know why.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Acknowledge the situation calmly - do not confirm or deny whether the ban was justified.
2. Explain that account restrictions are applied by the Trust and Safety team following a review.
3. Collect the player's Player ID and the date they first noticed the restriction.
4. Escalate to Trust and Safety with full details.
5. Inform the player they will receive a response within 48-72 hours.

**Do not:**
- Tell the player why you think they were banned
- Suggest the ban might be lifted
- Promise any specific outcome

---

## Q2: A player insists their ban was a mistake.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Acknowledge their frustration without taking a position on whether the ban was correct.
2. Explain that all ban appeals are reviewed by the Trust and Safety team.
3. Confirm their appeal has been logged and escalate.
4. Set realistic expectations - ban reviews can take 3-5 business days.

**Script:**
"I understand this is very frustrating, especially if you believe the restriction was applied in error. I'm not able to review the details of account restrictions at this level, but I've logged your appeal and escalated it to our Trust and Safety team. They will review your case and respond within 3-5 business days."

---

## Q3: A player is threatening legal action over a ban.

**Escalate immediately.** Do not engage with legal threats at Tier 1 level.

1. Acknowledge the message professionally.
2. Do not respond to the legal threat directly.
3. Escalate to Trust and Safety and flag as legal/compliance sensitive.
4. Inform the player their case has been escalated for specialist review.

---

## Q4: A player's account was banned but they claim it was hacked.

**Treat as high priority.**

1. Acknowledge both issues - the ban and the potential security breach.
2. Escalate to both Trust and Safety and the Security team simultaneously.
3. Collect: Player ID, date ban was noticed, date of suspected unauthorised access, any evidence.
4. Inform the player both issues will be reviewed together.
