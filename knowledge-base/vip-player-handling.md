# VIP Player Handling Guide

## Overview

VIP players are high-spend, long-tenure, or otherwise strategically important players who require a differentiated support experience. This guide defines how to identify VIP players and how to adjust handling accordingly.

---

## What Defines a VIP Player

VIP status may be indicated by:
- A VIP tag or flag in the player account record
- Lifetime spend above the defined threshold
- Long account tenure combined with consistent activity
- Direct referral from the player relations or business team

When in doubt, treat the player as VIP and confirm status with your team lead.

---

## How VIP Handling Differs

| Element | Standard Player | VIP Player |
|---|---|---|
| Response priority | Standard queue | Top of queue |
| First response time | Within SLA | Within 2 hours |
| Escalation speed | Standard | Immediate for any unresolved issue |
| Tone | Professional | Professional + elevated warmth |
| Resolution authority | Tier 1 scope only | Flag to senior agent for any gray areas |
| Follow-up | Only if player recontacts | Proactive follow-up after resolution |

---

## Tone Adjustments for VIP Players

Do not be sycophantic or over-apologetic. VIP players respond to efficiency and respect, not excessive flattery.

**Standard opening:**
"Thank you for reaching out. I can see this is an important issue and I want to make sure it is handled correctly for you."

**Avoid:**
"As one of our most valued players, we are deeply sorry..."

---

## VIP Escalation Protocol

1. Flag the ticket as VIP immediately upon identification.
2. Do not place a VIP player in a standard queue for complex issues.
3. Notify the senior agent or team lead of any VIP escalation.
4. Ensure the specialist team is aware of VIP status when escalating.
5. Follow up proactively after escalation to confirm the issue was resolved.

---

## VIP Complaints About Game Design or Policy

VIP players sometimes escalate complaints about game balance, spend value, or policy decisions.

- Acknowledge the concern seriously.
- Do not make exceptions to policy without authorization.
- Escalate to the player relations or senior management team if the complaint involves significant spend or threatens churn.
- Document all VIP complaints regardless of resolution.
