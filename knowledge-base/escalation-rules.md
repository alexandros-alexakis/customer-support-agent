# Escalation Rules

## Overview

This document defines when and how the agent escalates tickets 
to specialist teams. Escalation accuracy is a key performance 
metric for Tier 1 operations.

---

## Core Escalation Principle

When in doubt, escalate. A wrongly escalated ticket costs less 
than a wrongly handled one. Never attempt to resolve an issue 
outside Tier 1 scope to avoid escalating.

---

## Escalate Immediately - No Exceptions

The following issues must always be escalated without any 
attempt at Tier 1 resolution:

| Issue Type | Route To |
|---|---|
| Account ban or suspension | Trust & Safety team |
| Fraud or chargeback dispute | Billing specialist |
| Duplicate charges | Billing specialist |
| Suspected unauthorized account access | Security team |
| Legal or privacy requests (GDPR, data deletion) | Legal/Compliance |
| Player is abusive or threatening | Senior agent or TL |
| Bug affecting multiple players simultaneously | Technical team |

---

## Escalate After Tier 1 Steps Failed

The following issues should first go through Tier 1 resolution 
steps. If unresolved, escalate with full notes attached:

| Issue Type | Route To |
|---|---|
| Missing purchase items (verified transaction) | Billing specialist |
| Failed password reset | Account team |
| Guest account recovery | Account team |
| Persistent login issues after troubleshooting | Technical team |
| Store not loading after cache clear and reinstall | Technical team |
| Account merge or progress transfer request | Account specialist |
| Repeat contact on same unresolved issue | Senior agent |

---

## How to Escalate

1. Summarize the issue clearly in your escalation note.
2. Include all relevant details collected from the player:
   - Player ID / username
   - Device and OS version (if relevant)
   - Transaction IDs (if relevant)
   - Steps already attempted
   - Any screenshots or evidence provided
3. Set the correct priority level (standard / high / urgent).
4. Inform the player their case has been escalated and provide 
   a realistic response timeframe.

---

## Priority Levels

| Priority | When to Use | Expected Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| Urgent | Security breach, legal request, abusive player | Immediate |
| High | Duplicate charges, ban dispute, account breach | Within 4 hours |
| Standard | All other escalations | Within 24-48 hours |

---

## What Not to Do When Escalating

- Do not promise outcomes (refunds, item restoration, unban).
- Do not tell the player who specifically will handle their case.
- Do not escalate without collecting the minimum required information.
- Do not close the ticket before confirming escalation was received.
