# FAQ - Game Mechanics

## Overview

This document covers common player questions about in-game mechanics in strategy games. These are Tier 1 scope and should be resolved without escalation where possible.

---

## Q1: A player does not understand how a specific game feature works.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Ask the player to specify which feature they are confused about.
2. Provide a clear, concise explanation based on the knowledge base.
3. Direct them to the in-game tutorial or help section if available.
4. If the feature is not documented in the knowledge base, escalate to the game specialist team for a response.

**Do not** speculate on how features work if you are not certain.

---

## Q2: A player believes a game mechanic is unfair or broken.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Acknowledge their frustration without agreeing the mechanic is wrong.
2. Explain how the mechanic is designed to work.
3. If the player believes they experienced a bug (not just disagreement with the design), collect details and escalate to the technical team.
4. If it is a design complaint, acknowledge it and advise that feedback has been noted.

**Do not** promise that game mechanics will be changed based on player feedback.

---

## Q3: A player lost resources or progress due to what they believe is a game bug.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Collect Player ID, approximate time of loss, and what they were doing when it occurred.
2. Ask if they can reproduce the issue.
3. Escalate to the technical team with all details.
4. Inform the player that resource restoration is reviewed case by case and is not guaranteed.

**Do not** confirm or deny whether resources will be restored before specialist review.

---

## Q4: A player is asking about upcoming game content or features.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Do not share unconfirmed roadmap information.
2. Direct the player to official social media channels or the game's official news page for announcements.
3. Thank them for their interest.

**Do not** speculate on release dates or unannounced features.

---

## Q5: A player is asking how to find or join an alliance/guild.

**Resolution steps:**
1. Provide basic navigation instructions if documented.
2. Direct them to the in-game help section for detailed guides.
3. If the feature appears broken (cannot join despite meeting requirements), collect details and escalate to technical team.
